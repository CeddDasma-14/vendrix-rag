# Vendrix — Frequently Asked Questions

## Getting Started

**How long does setup take?**
Most customers run their first workflow within 30 minutes of signing up. Full team onboarding
(including training) typically takes 1–2 days. Growth and above plans include a guided onboarding
call with a Customer Success specialist.

**Do I need coding skills?**
No. The visual builder is designed for business users. For those who want to go deeper,
optional JavaScript snippets and direct API calls are supported.

**Can I migrate from Zapier or Make.com?**
Yes. We offer a one-click Zap importer for Zapier. Make.com scenarios can be converted using
our migration assistant. Most customers migrate in under a day.

## Technical Questions

**Is Vendrix secure?**
Yes. Vendrix is SOC 2 Type II certified. All data is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and
at rest (AES-256). We use OAuth for all integrations — we never store your credentials.
Enterprise customers can choose on-premise deployment.

**What is your uptime SLA?**
We guarantee 99.9% uptime on all paid plans. Our actual uptime over the past 12 months
is 99.97%. Enterprise plans can negotiate custom SLAs up to 99.99%.

**Can Vendrix handle high volumes?**
Yes. Our infrastructure auto-scales horizontally. Enterprise customers routinely process
millions of workflow runs per month with no performance degradation.

**What happens if a workflow fails?**
Our anomaly detection alerts you immediately via Slack or email with the exact step that
failed. Workflows can be set to retry automatically or pause for manual review.

## Billing & Plans

**Can I change plans anytime?**
Upgrades are effective immediately. Downgrades take effect at the end of your current
billing cycle.

**Do you offer annual discounts?**
Yes — annual billing saves 20% across all plans. Enterprise annual pricing is available
through our sales team.

**Is there a free plan?**
We offer a 14-day free trial with full Growth-plan access. No credit card required.
Your workflows are saved if you return after the trial ends.

**What counts as a workflow run?**
One run = one time a workflow executes from start to finish, regardless of how many
steps it contains.
