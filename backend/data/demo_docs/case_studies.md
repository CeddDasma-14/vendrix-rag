## Case Study 1: TechRecruit Co. — HR Automation

**Industry:** HR / Recruiting | **Plan:** Business | **Team Size:** 45 people

### Challenge
TechRecruit processed 500+ job applications per week manually. HR spent 15 hours/week
copying data between their ATS, email, and Slack — slowing their time-to-hire significantly.

### Solution
Built a Vendrix workflow that:
- Pulls new applications from their ATS automatically
- Uses AI to extract candidate data and tag by role and seniority
- Routes to the right hiring manager based on role rules
- Sends a personalised acknowledgment email to each candidate
- Posts a daily hiring pipeline summary to their Slack #hiring channel

### Results
- **87% reduction** in manual HR admin time
- Candidate response time cut by 2 days → offer acceptance rate up **23%**
- **$4,200/month** saved in admin overhead
- Full ROI achieved in **22 days**

## Case Study 2: InvoiceFlow Pro — Finance Automation

**Industry:** Financial Services | **Plan:** Growth | **Team Size:** 12 people

### Challenge
Manually processing 300+ invoices per month. Finance team spent 3 full days on data entry
alone. Late payment penalties were a recurring problem due to processing delays.

### Solution
Vendrix workflow that:
- Reads invoices from email using AI data extraction
- Cross-references with purchase orders in their ERP
- Auto-approves matching invoices under $5,000 (configurable threshold)
- Routes anomalies to the finance manager for review
- Syncs approved invoices to QuickBooks and triggers payment

### Results
- Invoice processing: 3 days/month → **4 hours/month**
- Payment accuracy: 94% → **99.2%**
- **Late payment penalties eliminated** — saving $800/month
- Finance team redirected to strategic work

## Case Study 3: RetailOps — Multi-Location Operations

**Industry:** Retail | **Plan:** Enterprise | **Team Size:** 200+ people

### Challenge
Managing inventory, supplier comms, and daily reporting across 18 locations.
Each location ran its own spreadsheet. Leadership had no unified visibility.

### Solution
Vendrix workflows across all 18 locations that:
- Sync inventory data daily into a central dashboard
- Trigger automatic supplier reorder requests when stock dips below threshold
- Generate and distribute a management report every morning at 7am
- Flag and escalate stock anomalies in real time

### Results
- Stockouts reduced by **67%** across all locations
- Management reporting fully automated — **0 hours/week** manual effort
- **$180,000/year** in operational cost savings
- Scaled from 18 to 31 locations **without adding any ops headcount**
