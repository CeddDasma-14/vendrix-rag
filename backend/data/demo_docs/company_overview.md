# Vendrix — Company Overview

## Founding Story
Vendrix was founded in 2019 by **Cedy Dasmarinas** (CEO) and **Daniel Cho** (CTO) in Austin, Texas.
Cedy previously led operations at a 500-person logistics firm and spent years manually
stitching together spreadsheets and email chains. Daniel built internal automation tooling
at two prior startups. They started Vendrix to give every operations team the leverage that
only enterprise companies could previously afford.

## Mission
Make intelligent workflow automation accessible to every B2B team — not just those with
dedicated engineering resources.

## Company Facts
- **Founded:** 2019
- **Headquarters:** Austin, Texas
- **Team size:** 85 employees across product, engineering, sales, and customer success
- **Funding:** Series B — $42M raised; investors include Emergence Capital and Bessemer Venture Partners
- **Customers:** 4,000+ companies in 60 countries

## Leadership Team
- **Cedy Dasmarinas** — CEO & Co-Founder. Former VP of Operations at Freight Logic Inc. Named Forbes 30 Under 30 (2022).
- **Daniel Cho** — CTO & Co-Founder. Ex-engineer at Rippling and Brex. Built Vendrix's core automation engine.
- **Priya Natarajan** — VP of Sales. Scaled sales orgs at two prior SaaS exits (Acquired by Salesforce, Workday).
- **James Whitfield** — VP of Customer Success. 10 years in enterprise SaaS onboarding and retention.

## Culture & Values
Vendrix operates on three principles: customer obsession, ruthless simplicity, and
transparency. The team is remote-first with hub offices in Austin and London.
Annual revenue crossed $12M ARR in 2023, growing 3x year-over-year.
