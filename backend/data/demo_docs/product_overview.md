# Vendrix — Workflow Automation Platform

## What is Vendrix?

Vendrix is an AI-powered workflow automation platform that helps B2B companies eliminate
repetitive manual work, reduce operational errors, and scale their teams without adding headcount.

## Core Features

### Visual Workflow Builder
- Drag-and-drop interface — no coding required
- AI-powered step suggestions based on your industry and existing workflows
- Conditional logic, loops, parallel branches, and error handling built in
- 300+ pre-built workflow templates across HR, Finance, Sales, and Operations

### Integrations
- Connects with 500+ tools: Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Jira, Notion, QuickBooks,
  Stripe, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Zendesk, and more
- REST API and webhooks for custom integrations
- Native compatibility with Zapier and Make.com for easy migration

### AI Capabilities
- Smart data extraction from emails, PDFs, and web forms
- Natural language triggers: "When a new enterprise lead arrives with budget > $50k..."
- Anomaly detection flags broken or slow workflows before they cause damage
- AI summarisation for long documents processed in workflows

### Team & Governance
- Role-based permissions and workflow ownership per team
- Full audit log for every automated action (who triggered what, when)
- Real-time monitoring dashboard with error alerts
- Slack and email notifications for workflow events

## Who Uses Vendrix?

- **Operations teams** — automate internal approvals, reporting, and data routing
- **Sales teams** — automate lead enrichment, routing, and follow-up sequences
- **Finance teams** — automate invoice processing, reconciliation, and approvals
- **HR teams** — automate onboarding, offboarding, and employee data syncs
